Frequently Asked Questions

How long does Ceramic Coating last?
Depending on the package you choose (CosmoCoat 9H), our coatings last between 2 to 5 years with proper maintenance. We provide a care guide upon completion.
Can you install PPF over vinyl wraps?
Yes! Installing PPF over a vinyl wrap is a great way to protect the finish and extend the life of the wrap, specifically for satin or matte finishes.
Do you offer a warranty?
Absolutely. All our PPF installations come with a 5-10 year warranty against yellowing and cracking. Our window tints come with a lifetime warranty against bubbling.
